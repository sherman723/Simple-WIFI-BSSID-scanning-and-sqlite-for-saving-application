					Situation
You would like to try to create an offline database of the Wi-Fi BSSID, 
Wi-Fi SSID and the geographical locations by wardriving method as what Google cars did few years ago:

1) Use the GPS function to locate the longitude and latitude.

2) Scan the Wi-Fi BSSID and SSID around

3) Save the data logged to a file/email






						What do the app do
	
	1.Display current latitude and longitude

	2.Scan the BSSID and SSID in surrounding using Wifi manager and display them in list view
	
	3.Use sqlite to provide save and open function.

	4.Able to send out the saved data through email application, with auto-fill in the address , data and title.
